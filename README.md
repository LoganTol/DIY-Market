🔨 DIY Market

Welcome to **DIY Market**, the premier peer-to-peer marketplace for home improvement, woodworking, and construction project plans! 

Whether you're a seasoned contractor looking to monetize your blueprints, or a DIY enthusiast searching for the perfect weekend project layout, DIY Market connects creators with builders to make every project a success.

---

## 🌟 Features

- **Explore Pre-Made Plans:** Browse through a variety of project plans ranging from simple bookshelves to complex backyard decks and sheds.
- **Sell Your Creations:** Upload and sell your custom plans, turning your past project blueprints into passive income.
- **Request Custom Blueprints:** Connect with expert contractors or designers to get a tailored plan created specifically for your unique home vision.
- **Vibrant Community:** Built for handy individuals, makers, and professional contractors to share their knowledge and skills.

## 💻 Tech Stack
This project is built using:
- **HTML5:** Semantic and accessible structure.
- **Vanilla CSS3:** Modern styling with Flexbox/Grid, custom variables, and responsive design.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Dynamic page rendering and interactivity without heavy frameworks.

## 🛠 Usage
Since this is a static frontend project, there's no complex build process natively required.
1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/LoganTol/DIY-Market.git`
2. Open `index.html` in your favorite web browser or use an extension like VSCode Live Server.
3. Start building!
